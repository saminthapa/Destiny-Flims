ABOUT US


Hello and welcome to Destiny Films. We are located in Bharatpur-7,Bikashchowk, Chitwan and provide photography and cinematography all over Nepal. Marriage is your precious or special treasure. These precious moments and memories should be cherished forever. We are an industry recognized team of photography and cinematography. We highlight your real moments as real memories. We are known as the destination for photography, cinematography for weddings and any other events or festivals. We do photography, cinematography photo framing, digital albums, graphic design as well as printing. Our Entertainment Film Production Division develops & produces film, television music production TV projects and executes publicity and marketing strategies on behalf of production companies as well as for individual directors,producers & writers. we also work with other talented producers , actors , video editors and other agents . We are expanding our services as our team grows to bring aboard and establish relationships with upcoming film producers, scriptwriters, film editors, and music composers. The company has been conceived to offer strategic planning, sales, marketing and consulting for all genres of films and television programs. It is hard enough to do good work at any time, harder now in a competitive commercial marketplace. Nevertheless, we firmly believe that our combination of passion, ingenuity and clear-cut honest responsiveness is the best way to ensure results that make a difference in the future of the industry.
